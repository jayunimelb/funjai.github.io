About Me

Welcome!! I am a third year PhD student in School of Physics, at the University of Melbourne, working in the observational cosmology group headed by Dr. Christian Reichardt. Prior to this I did my master's at IIT-Kharagpur under the supervision of Prof. Somnath Bharadwaj.
